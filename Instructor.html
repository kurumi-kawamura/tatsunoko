Instructor

Kendo Godan, Ryuichi Nagaura, is a graduate of Osaka University in Japan.


After graduation, he worked for a bit in the Kansai region before moving to – and finally settling in – Canada, where he continues to practice kendo at his dojo, Tatsunoko.

International Teaching Experience

Nagaura Sensei has participated in the Edinburgh Kendo Seminar as an assistant instructor. First in 2019 and again in 2023.
